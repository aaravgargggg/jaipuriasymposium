What We Are
"A crucible of minds.
A stage for the world."

Jaipuria Symposium is one of India's most prestigious student-run international debate events, bringing together the brightest young minds from across the globe for two days of intense intellectual discourse, diplomatic roleplay, and world-class debate.

Spanning multiple formats — from parliamentary debates to press corps and crisis committees — the Symposium challenges delegates to think critically, communicate powerfully, and lead with conviction. Held at Seth M. R. Jaipuria School, Lucknow, each edition raises the bar for what student-run events can achieve.
