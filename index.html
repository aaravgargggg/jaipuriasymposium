About the Symposium

Where young minds learn to shape the world.

Jaipuria Symposium 2026 is an inter-school Model UN and academic debate event hosted by Seth M. R. Jaipuria School, Lucknow. Designed for students of Classes 9 through 12, the Symposium brings together the sharpest minds from across India to debate, deliberate, and drive change.

Over two days, Participants will navigate real-world crises, draft landmark resolutions, and forge connections that last beyond the chambers. This is not just a competition — it is a crucible.
