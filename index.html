About the Symposium

Where young minds learn to shape the world.

In a city where conversation has long been an art, Lucknow’s legacy of tehzeeb and thoughtful discourse finds new expression in the Jaipuria Symposium. Rooted in a tradition that values depth over noise, the Symposium offers a space where dialogue is refined, not raised — where participants are encouraged not just to argue, but to understand, question, and engage with purpose.

Over two days, the Symposium unfolds through a progression of formats — from Oxford-style debates to Turncoat and Parliamentary rounds—each designed to test clarity, adaptability, and collaboration. More than a competition, it is a transformative experience where ideas evolve, perspectives deepen, and dialogue becomes a tool for insight, empathy, and meaningful change.
