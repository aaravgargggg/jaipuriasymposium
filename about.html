About Us

More Than a Debate.
A Movement.

Jaipuria Symposium is built on the belief that the most important conversations about the world's future should begin with its youth.

Our Manifesto

We live in a world defined by complexity — where no challenge exists in isolation, and no solution is simple. Climate change, geopolitical tension, inequality, and technological disruption demand leaders who can think across disciplines, listen across divides, and speak with conviction.

Jaipuria Symposium exists to forge those leaders. Every committee session, every resolution, every crisis update is a crucible — pushing delegates to go beyond what they thought they were capable of.

We don't just simulate the United Nations. We simulate the world.

What We Stand For

Three Pillars of
Symposium

01

Diplomacy

Understanding that every position has context, every conflict has history, and every solution requires negotiation across difference.

02

Leadership

Developing the capacity to lead not through authority, but through clarity of thought, strength of character, and depth of preparation.

03

Global Discourse

Creating a space where voices from across the world meet, debate, challenge, and ultimately build understanding that transcends borders.
